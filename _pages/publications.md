## Communications & Presentations

#### June 2026 — Future Earth Sustainability Science Conference, Lausanne 🇨🇭



<div style="display: flex; align-items: flex-start; gap: 1.5em; margin-bottom: 1.5em;">
  <img src="/equals/assets/img/EQUALS/conferences/future_earth_2026.png"

    <strong>Operationalizing Energy Justice: Social Sciences and Quantitative Modeling for energy planning</strong>
    <p>Arthur Clerjon, Emmanuelle Santoire, and Justine Duval co-convened an interdisciplinary workshop
    at the <a href="https://pathways.futureearth.org/sustainability-science-conference/" target="_blank">2026 Annual Sustainability Science Conference</a>
    (University of Lausanne, 2–5 June 2026), organized by Future Earth Pathways.</p>
    <p>The session brought together expertise in energy system modeling, energy geography, and resource
    analysis to explore how energy justice can be operationalized in quantitative planning tools.
    Participants engaged in real-time modeling exercises to examine trade-offs between technological
    choices, resource constraints, and equity considerations.</p>
